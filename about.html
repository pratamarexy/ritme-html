About Us

A trusted name since 1989, PT Ritme IndahKreasi has completed dwelling houses, offices, shops, hospitals, hotels and banks. As a Consultant & builder, we strive to cater client’s needs throughout the entire design & build processes. We offer a wide range of services in Architecture, Building, Interior design & Furniture.

We have extensive amount of experiences, knowledge & resources to complete each project with enthusiasm thus resulted in quality.

Professionalism, innovation, dedication are our objectives. Our goal is to pursue those values in order to deliver client’s satisfaction.

Vision & Mission

Being the best & trusted contractor to our clients, by delivering top quality services, cost effective and excellence in end finished products, architecture, construction & custom furniture design.

Maintain strong relationships with our clients, suppliers and employees. Provides a safe working environment for all employees. Invest in the latest equipment and machineries to achieve speed and quality service.
